GLASSES Our cutting-edge glasses, powered by AI/ML and sensors, provide a remarkable solution for those with partial blindness. They enable wearers to see a magnified and highly detailed view of their surroundings on the QLED panel. This innovative technology enhances visual clarity and independence, making daily activities more accessible for individuals with partial blindness.
GLOVES Employing an array of sensors integrated into gloves connected to speakers, our innovative system harnesses continuous deep learning algorithms to bridge communication gaps. It translates the gestures and signs of both British and American Sign Language into spoken words, instantly vocalizing the user's communication. This technology empowers individuals to engage in seamless conversations, promoting inclusivity and enhancing their ability to express themselves effectively.
